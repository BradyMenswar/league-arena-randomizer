League of Legends Arena Randomizer

This tool will let you roll for a random champion, itemset, and augment path to take for the 2v2v2v2 arena mode.

Rules

  • You can only buy the items in the shown build from left to right.
  • If you make it to a point where you can afford to sell your starting items or boots, you should replace them with the final two items shown.
  • You must max out the shown ability first before maxing any other abilities.
  • The augments shown in the build show L for left, C for center, and R for right to determine which card to pick.
  • You can reroll augments, but you still have to pick the card the build says.
  • Do not select the augment "Mythical". If it is what the build shows to get, pick the augment to the left of it instead.
  • If you do not own the shown champion, reroll until you find one that you own.
  • You may purchase potions if you so choose.
